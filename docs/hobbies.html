What I Love Doing

Stargazing & Space Dreaming

There’s something humbling about staring at the night sky and realizing how tiny we are. I like to imagine where humanity will go next.

Coding

It’s like solving puzzles that actually come to life. I enjoy creating small projects that make my life easier—or just for fun.

Swimming

Water clears my head better than any coffee. It’s the one place where I can completely disconnect.

Cooking

I like experimenting in the kitchen. Sometimes it’s amazing, sometimes it’s a disaster, but it’s always fun.

Reading Fantasy Books

Good stories make me forget about time. Harry Potter is a classic, but I’m always on the hunt for the next great world to dive into.

DIY Rocket Models

I build small rockets just to see how things work. It’s half science, half playing around—but that’s how you learn.
