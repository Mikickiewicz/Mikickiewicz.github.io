My Hobbies

Things that keep me curious and inspired.

What I Love Doing

Volleyball (H12 Team, Delft Punch)

I love playing volleyball with friends — it’s energetic, team-based, and always keeps me motivated to move.

Stargazing & Space Dreaming

There’s something humbling about staring at the night sky and realizing how tiny we are. I like to imagine where humanity will go next.

Coding

I like coding, especially doing challenges on Codewars. It’s fun to solve tricky problems and see if I can figure them out.

Swimming & Road Cycling

Swimming helps me relax and clear my head a bit. I’m not super serious about it — I just like getting some movement in every few days. Sometimes I switch it up and go for a ride on my road bike instead.

Cooking

Once in a blue moon, I like to cook something special.

Reading Fantasy Books

Right now I’m reading The Desert Spear from the Demon Cycle series by Peter V. Brett. It’s full of demons, magic, and epic adventures.
